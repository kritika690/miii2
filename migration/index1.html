Our Vision

The American Institute will be nurturing, safe and professional environment that supports the education success and social, emotional and physical development of all the students. Courses will be focused on the learner. All staff will be qualified and caring instructors who are attentitive to the educational, cultural and physical needs of students. Parents will be positive and supporting members of the institution. Students will be respectful self-disciplined, productive citizens who think critically to make decisions and act ethically.

Our Values

1. Our institution always promotes Honesty and encourage staff and students to express their opinions accurately by which we get ample scope for improvisation.
2. Students and staff are encouraged to do discussions to generate innovative ideas.
3. We always acknowledge, appreciate and encourage dedication of/among staff and students.
4. We put constant efforts to keep institution hygienic and always promote personal hygiene among students.

Why choose The American Institute?

At American Institute, we understand the need of quality education in the growing world that offers a cut-throat competition. We offer best quality of education because we believe that better services are the key to success. Our continued progress is projected by our students who work hard in order to fulfill their goals and bring glory to the institution.
